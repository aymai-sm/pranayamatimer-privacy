Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use Voice Start?
Turn on "Voice start" in the app, tap "Listen for 'Start Practice,'" then say the phrase "start practice" within 20 seconds. The app will begin automatically once it recognizes the phrase.

Why isn't Voice Start or Voice Count working?
Make sure microphone and speech recognition permissions are enabled for the app in your device's Settings, and that your device isn't in silent/mute mode for voice output.

Does the app work offline?
Yes — timing, voice cues, and presets all work without an internet connection. Voice recognition for "Voice start" may use on-device or Apple's speech recognition service depending on your device.
