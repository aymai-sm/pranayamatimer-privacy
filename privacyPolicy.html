Privacy Policy — Aym Pranayama Timer

Last updated: July 2026

Aym Pranayama Timer is a personal breathing-practice timer. This policy explains what data the app uses and how.

Data Collection

Aym Pranayama Timer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party. The app has no backend server and does not create user accounts.

Microphone and Speech Recognition

If you enable the optional "Voice start" feature, the app uses your device's microphone and Apple's Speech framework to listen for the spoken phrase "start practice." Audio is processed using Apple's on-device or Apple-hosted speech recognition service, subject to Apple's own privacy policies. The app itself does not record, store, or transmit this audio anywhere. Microphone access is only active while you have explicitly enabled Voice Start and are actively using it.

Voice Output

The app uses your device's built-in text-to-speech to announce breathing cues ("In," "Out," "Hold," etc.). This is entirely on-device and involves no data transmission.

Analytics and Advertising

This app contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising of any kind.
